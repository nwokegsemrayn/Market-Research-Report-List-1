<p><p>The 5G indoor small base station market can be categorized into two primary segments: Sub-6GHz and mmWave. Sub-6GHz focuses on lower-frequency bands, offering broader coverage and better penetration through obstacles, making it ideal for urban and indoor environments. In contrast, mmWave utilizes higher frequencies, delivering significantly faster speeds and greater capacity, but with limited range and reduced penetration capabilities. Both types serve distinct needs—Sub-6GHz for widespread accessibility and mmWave for high-density applications requiring enhanced data throughput.</p></p>
